My name is Jennefer and I am a student at University of Limerick. My background is in business but I always loved everything related to design, photography and fashion. I always loved to use my creativity while working.

I worked as a designer for a while and have found my passion. A few years ago I started to read about UX Design and the importance of thinking about the user more than if a screen looks pretty or not, so when I saw the Master degree at the UL I decided to improve my knowledge and here I am.

I want to talk with people, understand their needs and create solutions to their problems.
